Research Facilities

State-of-the-art laboratory infrastructure for advanced material processing and characterization.

Supercritical Dryer

Critical for aerogel synthesis, allowing for the removal of solvents without collapsing the highly porous structure of the material.

Freeze Dryer

Enables lyophilization process for preserving biological samples and creating porous materials through ice sublimation.

Thermal Conductivity Meter

High-precision measurement systems to characterize the thermal insulation and transport properties of new materials.

Density Meter (Bulk & TAP)

Specialized equipment for measuring the bulk density and tapped density of powders and granules.

Gas Pycnometer

Uses gas displacement to determine the true skeletal volume and density of solid materials and powders.

Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM)

Advanced imaging for micro-structural analysis and topography of fiber networks and aerogel surfaces.

Vacuum Oven

Provides a controlled, oxygen-free environment for thermal treatment and drying of sensitive materials.

Impedance Tube

Used for sound insulation studies to measure absorption coefficients and acoustic transmission loss.

Ball Milling

Mechanical processing equipment for particle size reduction and homogeneous mixing of raw materials.

High Shear Homogenizer

High-energy mechanical shear for creating stable emulsions and fine dispersions in chemical processing.

Thermal Imaging Camera

Non-contact thermal mapping for evaluating the performance of insulation materials and heat distribution.

Liquid Density Meter

Precision instrument for the rapid and accurate measurement of liquid density and concentration.
